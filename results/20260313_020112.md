## Welcome to SeaPortal

The intelligent web content extraction tool designed for the age of AI agents and automation.

SeaPortal is an open-source tool for extracting clean, readable content from any web page. Built with Go, it handles everything from simple blogs to complex single-page applications. Our mission is to make web content accessible to AI agents and automation tools.
